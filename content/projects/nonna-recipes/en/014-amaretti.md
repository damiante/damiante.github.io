# Amaretti (Italian Almond Cookies)

## Ingredients
- 100 grams ground almonds (or half kilogram)
- 100 grams sugar (or 250 grams)
- 1 egg white (or multiple egg whites depending on quantity)
- ½ cup ground apricots (optional)

## Instructions
1. Beat the egg whites well
2. Then add the sugar
3. Then mix everything together (almonds and apricots if used)
4. Form into small balls
5. Oven at 300-320°F (160°C) for 15-20 minutes (or 25 minutes)

**NOTE:** There are two variants of this recipe with different quantities.
