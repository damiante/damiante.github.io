# Pickled Onions

## Ingredients
- ½ gallon vinegar
- 2 pounds sugar
- ½ dozen cloves
- Peppercorns (1 teaspoon)
- 1 teaspoon mixed spice
- 2 tablespoons salt
- Small onions (quantity not specified)

## Instructions
1. Boil vinegar, sugar, cloves, peppercorns, mixed spice, and salt together for 5 minutes
2. Strain and let cool
3. When cold, add the onions
4. Close the jar tightly

## Notes
**Brumea** likely refers to the jar or container for pickling.
