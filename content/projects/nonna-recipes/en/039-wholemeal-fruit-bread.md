# Wholemeal Fruit Bread

## Ingredients
- 2 cups wholemeal self-raising flour
- ½ cup coconut
- 1 cup yoghurt
- 125 grams margarine
- 2 tablespoons honey
- ½ cup bran
- ½ cup raisins
- 2 tablespoons sunflower seeds
- 1 cup dried apricots (cooked)
- 10 prunes
- ½ cup currants
- Water (as needed)
- ½ cup skim milk powder

## Instructions
Instructions not fully specified in the transcription. This appears to be an ingredients list for a healthy wholemeal fruit bread.

1. Mix dry ingredients together
2. Add wet ingredients (yoghurt, margarine, honey, water)
3. Fold in fruits and nuts
4. Bake until done
