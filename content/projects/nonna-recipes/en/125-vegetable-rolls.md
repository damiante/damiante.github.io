# Involtini / Vegetable Rolls

## Ingredients

### For the Filling
- 1 onion
- 100 grams green beans
- 100 grams carrots

### For the Pastry
- 100 grams sugar
- 100 grams butter
- 200 grams flour
- Grated lemon zest

## Method

1. Boil the green beans slightly
2. Sauté in a pan with the carrots cut into thin long strips
3. Let cool in a colander
4. When cold, roll them in pasta frolla (shortcrust pastry)
5. Fry until golden

### For the Pasta Frolla
1. Mix sugar, butter, flour, and grated lemon zest
2. Roll out thin
3. Cut into strips
4. Wrap around the cooked vegetables
5. Fry at 200°C for 10 minutes or deep fry

**Oven Temperature:** 200°C (if baking) or deep fry
