# Cookies ca **Lifci**

## Ingredients

### For the cookies:
- 2½ cups sugar
- 4 pounds butter
- 12 eggs
- 2 cups warm milk
- 2 teaspoons baking soda and **areinzo** (probably "essence")
- Vanilla
- 5 pounds flour

### For the cream:
- 2 pounds **arisanza** (probably icing sugar) and chocolate
- A bit of hot water

## Instructions

### Cookies:
1. Mix butter and sugar
2. Add the eggs one by one
3. Then add the milk, baking soda, **areinzo**, and vanilla
4. And then the flour

### Cream:
1. Mix **arisanza** and chocolate with a bit of hot water
