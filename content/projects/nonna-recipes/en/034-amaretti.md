# Amaretti

## Ingredients
- 100 grams almonds
- ½ small glass **biccoli** (likely another type of nut or measurement)
- 100 grams sugar
- 1 egg white

## Instructions
1. Grind the almonds and **biccoli**
2. Beat the egg whites well
3. Add the sugar and beat
4. Mix everything together
5. Shape into cookies
6. Bake at 300°F (160°C) for 15 to 20 minutes
