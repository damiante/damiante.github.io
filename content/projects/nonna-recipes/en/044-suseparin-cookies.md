# Suseparin Cookies

## Ingredients
- 1 cup oil
- 4⅓ cups white sugar
- 5 eggs
- Lemon essence
- Self-raising flour (as much as it takes)
- Raisins for rolling

## Instructions
1. Beat the eggs and sugar first
2. Add everything else
3. Add the flour slowly, little by little
4. Put raisins on a plate
5. Take with a spoon and roll in your hand
6. Roll in the raisins
7. Bake in a very hot oven at 330°F for 8 to 10 minutes
