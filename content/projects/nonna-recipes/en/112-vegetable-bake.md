# Disci di Vegetabili / Vegetable Bake

## Ingredients

- 6 zucchini
- 1½ potatoes
- Mozzarella
- Tomato sauce
- Oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Slice and fry the zucchini
2. Boil the potatoes
3. Grease a baking dish with a little butter
4. Layer the ingredients: start with a layer of zucchini, then a layer of sliced potatoes, a little mozzarella cut into slices, and a little tomato sauce
5. Continue layering until all ingredients are used
6. Top with mozzarella
7. Bake until the cheese is melted and golden

**Alternate Filling:** You can stuff zucchini flowers with this mixture: boiled and mashed potatoes, fresh tomatoes cut into small pieces, and grated cheese. Bake with cheese slices on top.
