# Orange Cream

## Ingredients
- 2 cups water
- 1 cup sugar (or 1½ cups depending on taste)
- 1 cup orange juice
- A little cinnamon
- 1½ tablespoons cornflour
- ½ tablespoon custard powder
- 1 or 2 tablespoons sugar

## Instructions
1. Put water and sugar in a pot
2. When it boils, add 1 cup of orange juice
3. Add a little cinnamon
4. Add 1½ tablespoons of cornflour and ½ tablespoon of custard powder
5. Add 1 or 2 tablespoons of sugar
6. Spread it on ladyfinger cookies and put them in the freezer
