# Torta di Frutta Secca / Dried Fruit Cake

## Ingredients

- 125 grams hazelnuts, roasted
- 90 grams slivered almonds
- 125 grams dried figs
- 90 grams dark chocolate
- 125 grams mixed peel
- 3 eggs
- ½ cup rum
- 1½ cups self-raising flour
- Sugar (amount not specified)
- Zest of lemon and orange

## Instructions

1. Cut everything into small pieces (hazelnuts, chocolate, figs, mixed peel)
2. Mix the nuts, chocolate, and citrus zests together
3. Beat eggs and sugar very well
4. Mix everything together with the egg mixture
5. Add the flour and rum
6. Mix well
7. Pour into a greased dish
8. Bake for 1 hour at moderate temperature (not too hot)
9. When cooled, slice into thin slices

**Oven Temperature:** Moderate (approximately 160-170°C)
