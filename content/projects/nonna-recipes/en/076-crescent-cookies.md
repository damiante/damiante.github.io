# Biscotti a Cornetto di Turrona / Crescent Cookies

## Ingredients

- 250 grams plain flour
- 125 grams butter, melted
- 1 egg
- A little salt
- 1 packet yeast
- Vanilla essence

### For the Filling
- Jam (marmalade)
- Ground almonds (optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix flour, melted butter, egg, salt, yeast, and vanilla essence together
2. Knead into a smooth dough
3. Roll out the dough
4. Cut into triangles
5. Place a little jam in the middle of each triangle
6. You can also add ground almonds to the filling
7. Roll into crescents (cornetti)
8. Bake at 180°C until golden

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
