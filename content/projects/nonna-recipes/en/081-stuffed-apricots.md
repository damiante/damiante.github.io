# Apricot Ripieni / Stuffed Apricots

## Ingredients

- Fresh apricots
- Ground almonds
- Pine nuts
- Walnuts
- Melted chocolate (for topping)

## Instructions

1. Cut the apricots in half and remove the pits
2. Grind together almonds, pine nuts, and walnuts
3. Fill the apricot halves with the nut mixture
4. Bake until the apricots are softened
5. Melt chocolate in a double boiler
6. Drizzle or spread a little melted chocolate on top of each stuffed apricot
7. Serve warm or at room temperature
