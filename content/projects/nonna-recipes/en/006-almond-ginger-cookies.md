# Almond Ginger Cookies

## Ingredients
- 180 grams butter
- ⅔ cup brown sugar
- ¼ teaspoon almond essence
- 1 egg, lightly beaten
- ½ cup self-raising flour
- 1 cup plain flour
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- ¾ cup toasted muesli
- ¼ cup slivered almonds
- ¼ cup chopped raisins

## Instructions
1. Beat butter, sugar, and essence
2. Gradually beat in egg
3. Fold in all dry ingredients
4. Mix well
5. Place level tablespoons on oven trays
