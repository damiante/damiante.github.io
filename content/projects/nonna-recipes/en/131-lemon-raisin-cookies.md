# Basotti di Nuana / Lemon Raisin Cookies

## Ingredients

- 300 grams self-raising flour
- 1 egg
- 100 grams butter
- 90 grams sugar
- ½ teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Grated zest of 1 lemon
- Abundant raisins (sultanas)

## Instructions

1. Beat sugar and butter until light and fluffy
2. Add the egg, lemon juice, and lemon zest
3. Mix the bicarbonate of soda with the flour
4. Add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients after adding the egg and lemon juice
5. Add the flour a little at a time
6. Add abundant raisins
7. Let rest for 30 minutes
8. Shape as desired
9. Roll in abundant raisins to coat
10. Bake at 190°C for 15 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 190°C
