# Pizzettone / Sweet Lemon Cake

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- ¾ cup sugar
- 180 grams oil
- 130 grams milk
- 250 grams self-raising flour
- 1 packet vanilla
- Grated lemon zest

## Instructions

1. Beat eggs and sugar together
2. Add oil and milk, mix well
3. Add flour, vanilla, and grated lemon zest
4. Mix until smooth
5. Pour into a greased cake pan
6. Bake at 170°C for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until a skewer inserted comes out clean

**Oven Temperature:** 170°C
