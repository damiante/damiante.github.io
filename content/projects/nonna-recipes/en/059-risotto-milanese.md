# Risotto Milanese

## Ingredients
- A little oil
- Raw rice (quantity not specified)
- 1 glass white wine (optional, not necessary)
- Prepared broth (quantity as needed)
- A little **peona** (likely bacon or pancetta), cut into small pieces (optional)
- Grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions
1. Put a little oil in the pan
2. Heat the pan
3. Add the raw rice and stir for a little while
4. Add a glass of white wine (optional, not necessary)
5. Add prepared broth (already made)
6. If you like, you can add a little **peona** cut into small pieces
7. Add grated Parmesan cheese
8. Continue cooking until rice is done
