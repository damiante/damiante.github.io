# Carrot Cake

## Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup parsley flour (likely **prezzemolata** - sifted flour)
- 2 cups grated carrots
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
- 1 tablespoon or glass **piuvere** (baking powder?)
- 2 ounces butter
- 100 grams walnuts

## Instructions
1. Beat the eggs well
2. Mix everything together
