# Torta di Pesche / Peach Tart

## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram soft white peaches
- 150 grams sugar
- A little Marsala or sweet sherry
- 6 eggs
- A little melted chocolate or sugar (for topping)

## Instructions

1. Peel and core the peaches
2. Beat the peaches with the sugar and a little Marsala or sweet sherry
3. Puree the peaches until smooth
4. Add 6 eggs and beat well
5. Pour the mixture into a dish lined with parchment paper
6. Place the dish in a water bath (bain-marie)
7. Bake at 180°C for 35 minutes
8. When done, sprinkle a little sugar on top or drizzle with melted chocolate

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
