# Passion Fruit Cake and Crescent Cookies

## Passion Fruit Cake (Torta)

### Ingredients
- 100 grams butter
- 1 cup caster sugar
- 2 **baspocra** (tablespoons) grated lemon zest
- 4 eggs (add one at a time)
- 1½ cups self-raising flour
- ½ cup passion fruit
- ½ cup milk

### Instructions
1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add lemon zest
3. Add eggs one at a time
4. Add self-raising flour
5. Add passion fruit and milk
6. Bake at 150°C

## Crescent Cookies (Biscotti)

### Ingredients
- 150 grams ground almonds
- 200 grams butter
- 150 grams caster sugar

### Instructions
1. Mix together ground almonds, butter, and caster sugar
2. Grind them together
3. Add a little more ground almonds
4. Work it a little
5. Shape into crescents (half-moon)
6. Roll in icing sugar
7. Bake for 12 minutes
