# Ricotta Pura / Ricotta Pie

## Ingredients

- 200 grams flour
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 200 grams ricotta
- 3 egg whites
- 2 egg yolks
- A little milk

## Instructions

1. Beat the ricotta with the eggs (whites and yolks)
2. Add sugar and mix well
3. Add flour gradually
4. Add a little milk to adjust consistency
5. Pour into a greased pie dish (similar to apple pie preparation)
6. Bake in a very hot oven for 20 minutes
