# Patate Grattuggiate / Potato Fritters

## Ingredients

- Potatoes, grated
- A little cream
- 1 egg
- A little grated cheese (optional)

## Instructions

1. Grate the potatoes
2. Mix with a little cream and 1 egg
3. Add a little grated cheese if desired
4. Form into patties or shapes
5. Fry until golden and crispy on both sides
