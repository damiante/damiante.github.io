# Good Almond Cookies

## Ingredients
- 1 kilogram ground almonds (or 1.1 kg according to Angela)
- 1 kilogram sugar (or 750g caster sugar according to Angela)
- 10 egg whites (or 6-8 according to other versions)
- Can also add 2 yolks
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- A little almond essence
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 2 tablespoons **spraca** (unclear dialect word)
- Vanilla

## Instructions
1. Mix sugar and almonds together
2. Beat the egg whites well
3. Add the sugar
4. Add two packets of vanilla sugar
5. Add 2 tablespoons of baking powder
6. Mix the almonds with a little **spraca**
7. Add 2 tablespoons of honey
8. Grind them twice with the machine before adding the honey
9. Then roll them in cornflour
10. Oven at 300°F/350°F for 12-13 minutes (or 180°C)

**NOTE:** This recipe has several variants. Angela's version uses slightly different proportions.
