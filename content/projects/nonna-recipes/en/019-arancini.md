# Arancini (Sicilian Rice Balls)

## Ingredients
- 1½ kilograms rice
- 200 grams grated cheese
- 100 grams bread crumbs
- 100 grams peas
- 125 grams butter
- 2 whole eggs
- ½ cup milk
- Parsley

### For the filling:
- Mozzarella
- Peas
- **Biclinia** (probably "ground meat" or "besciamella" - bechamel)
- Sauce made without meat

## Instructions
1. Cook the rice
2. Mix with grated cheese, butter, eggs, and milk
3. In the middle put mozzarella, peas, **biclinia** with a little sauce
4. Form the arancini balls
5. Roll in bread crumbs
6. Fry

### Yield notes:
- 750 grams of rice makes 32 arancini
