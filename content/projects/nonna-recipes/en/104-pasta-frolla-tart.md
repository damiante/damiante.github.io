# Pasta Frolla / Shortcrust Pastry Tart

## Ingredients

- 200 grams flour
- 80 grams butter
- 50 grams sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- A little salt
- Grated lemon zest

### For the Filling
- 250 grams raisins (sultanas)
- 50 grams sugar
- Grated lemon zest

## Instructions

1. Mix flour, butter, sugar, egg, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest to make the pastry
2. Roll out the dough and line a tart pan
3. For the filling, mix raisins with sugar and grated lemon zest
4. Fill the pastry shell with the raisin mixture
5. Bake until golden
