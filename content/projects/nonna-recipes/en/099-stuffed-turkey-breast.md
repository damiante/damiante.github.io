# Petto di Tachino Ripieni / Stuffed Turkey Breast

## Ingredients

- Turkey breast, cut large and wide
- 250 grams dried prunes
- 250 grams walnuts
- 250 grams boiled and peeled chestnuts
- 2 tablespoons brandy
- Béchamel sauce

## Instructions

1. Cut all the dried fruit (prunes, walnuts, chestnuts) into small pieces
2. Soak the fruit in 2 tablespoons of brandy
3. Cut the turkey breast large and wide so it can be filled
4. Fill the turkey breast with the fruit and nut mixture
5. Cover the top with béchamel sauce
6. Roll up and tie with kitchen string
7. Place in the oven at 200°C for 30 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 200°C
