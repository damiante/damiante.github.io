# Swedish Ice Cream

## Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- 1/50 plus water mixture (probably icing sugar mixture)
- 180 grams icing mixture
- 2 tablespoons flavor:
  - Coffee or
  - 2 bananas or
  - Similar
- 300ml whipping cream

## Instructions
1. Separate the whites from the yolks
2. Whip the egg whites until they stay in the bowl when you turn it upside down
3. Mix the sugar into the egg whites gradually
4. Blend the egg yolks with the flavor
5. Then mix the flavor yolk mixture into the egg whites
6. Whip the cream halfway, until it just moves in the bowl
7. Mix cream with the rest
8. Put everything in a 1Â½ liter bowl
9. Put into the freezer for at least 3 hours
10. Then eat / enjoy
