# Biscotti con Mandorle e Miele / Almond and Honey Cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram almonds
- 1½ kilograms honey
- 1½ kilograms plain flour
- 1 tablespoon bicarbonate of soda

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Form into round logs
3. Bake at 160°C until set
4. Remove from oven and let cool slightly
5. Slice into biscotti (slices)
6. Return to oven to toast the cut sides

**Oven Temperature:** 160°C
