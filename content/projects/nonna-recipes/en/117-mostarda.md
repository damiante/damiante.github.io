# Mostarda / Grape Must Custard

## Ingredients

- Fresh grape must (mosto)
- 100 grams flour or cornflour per litre of must (use half of each)

## Method

1. Boil the must
2. The next day, strain it through a cloth
3. For each litre of strained must, add 100 grams of flour (or use 50 grams cornflour and 50 grams plain flour)
4. Mix the flour in when the must is cold
5. Heat and stir continuously like making custard until thickened

**Note:** This traditional Sicilian sweet is made during grape harvest season.
