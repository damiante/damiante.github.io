# Torta di Ricotta e Ciliegie / Ricotta and Cherry Tart

## Ingredients

### For the Base
- 350 grams ricotta
- 300 grams sugar
- 30 grams almonds
- 30 grams hazelnuts
- 120 grams dry biscuits (cookies)
- 80 grams butter
- 250 grams cream

### For the Filling
- 2 egg yolks
- 100 grams sugar dissolved in a little water
- A little liqueur
- A little gelatin
- Fresh cherries

## Instructions

1. Crush 4-5 biscuits with the ground almonds and hazelnuts to make the base
2. Mix with melted butter and press into the bottom of a pan
3. Refrigerate for 6 hours
4. Beat the egg yolks with a little liqueur
5. Add 100 grams of sugar dissolved in a little water
6. Add a little gelatin to help it set
7. Mix in the ricotta
8. Place a layer of cherries in the base
9. Pour the ricotta filling over the cherries
10. Top with remaining cherries
11. Refrigerate until set
