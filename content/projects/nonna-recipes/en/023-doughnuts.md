# Doughnuts

## Ingredients
- 4 cups plain flour
- 4 eggs
- 2 ounces butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1 inch square of yeast
- A pinch of salt

## Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Let rise until the dough has doubled in size
3. Shape into round doughnuts using a **broghtone** (rolling pin or doughnut cutter)
4. Fry until golden brown
