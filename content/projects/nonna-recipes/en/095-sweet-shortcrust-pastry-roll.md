# Pasta Frolla Dolce / Sweet Shortcrust Pastry Roll

## Ingredients

- 2 eggs plus 1 egg yolk
- 500 grams flour
- 150 grams butter
- 1 glass liqueur
- Jam or marmalade

## Instructions

1. Mix eggs, egg yolk, flour, butter, and liqueur together to make a dough
2. Knead until smooth
3. Roll out the dough thin like lasagna sheets
4. Spread jam or marmalade over the surface
5. Roll up tightly into a log
6. Brush with a little milk
7. Bake at 170°C for 45 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 170°C
