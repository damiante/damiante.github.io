# Fruit Checco

## Version 1 (Traditional)

### Ingredients
- 1 pound sultanas
- 1 pound cherries
- ½ pound mixed fruit
- 1 pound flour (half plain, half self-raising)
- 2 ounces nuts (walnuts)
- 1 tablespoon mixed spice
- 1 tablespoon nutmeg
- 1 pound sugar
- 1 pound butter
- 8 eggs
- Brandy for soaking

### Instructions
1. Soak the fruit in brandy for a few days beforehand
2. Mix sugar and butter well
3. Add eggs one by one
4. Add the flour
5. Add the soaked fruit
6. Bake until done

## Version 2 (Modern)

### Ingredients
- 125 grams butter or margarine, softened
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup plain flour
- 1 cup self-raising flour
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- 1 cup chopped dates
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 2 medium green apples, peeled and chopped into small pieces

### Instructions
1. Beat butter until soft
2. Add sugar and beat well
3. Add eggs one by one and beat well
4. Add cinnamon, ginger, and baking powder
5. Add flours and mix
6. Fold in dates, walnuts, and apples
7. Bake at specified temperature (not included in transcription)
