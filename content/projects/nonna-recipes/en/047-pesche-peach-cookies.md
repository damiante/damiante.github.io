# Pesche (Peach Cookies)

## Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- 100 grams butter, melted
- 200 grams sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- Flour (plain) - quantity not specified

### Filling
- Cream filling
- A little white vermouth

### Coating
- **Zorso** (likely pink/peach coloring)
- Sugar

## Instructions
1. Beat the egg whites first
2. Add the egg yolks
3. Add the sugar and beat
4. Add baking powder
5. Add the melted butter
6. Shape into small balls
7. Bake at 250Â°F
8. After removing from oven, **evida la parta un po dura** (remove the harder part)
9. Fill with cream
10. Add a little white vermouth
11. Add a little coloring (**zorso**) in a cup
12. Dip in the coloring
13. Roll in sugar
14. Place on a small plate

## Notes
The continuation mentions **baquanto di dentro con caffe prima di mettere la crema** (dip the inside with coffee before adding the cream).
