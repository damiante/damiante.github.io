# Crostoli di Ricotta e Spinaci / Ricotta and Spinach Pie

## Ingredients

### For the Pastry
- 600 grams flour
- 250 grams butter
- 25 grams salt
- ½ teaspoon sugar
- Lukewarm water for kneading

### For the Filling
- 500 grams ricotta
- 400 grams spinach
- 3 eggs plus 1 egg yolk
- 40 grams sultanas (raisins)
- 40 grams almonds, cut into pieces
- 30 grams pine nuts
- 30 grams Parmesan cheese
- 1 egg white (for brushing)

## Instructions

1. Make the pastry by mixing flour, butter, salt, sugar, and lukewarm water
2. Knead until smooth
3. For the filling, mix ricotta, spinach, eggs, egg yolk, sultanas, almonds, pine nuts, and Parmesan
4. Roll out the pastry and line a baking dish
5. Fill with the ricotta and spinach mixture
6. Make strips of the same pastry and arrange on top in a lattice pattern
7. Brush the top with egg white
8. Bake at 370°F (190°C) for 35 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 370°F (190°C)
