# Pain **Chich** and Panettone

## Pain **Chich** (Crepes)

### Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- 225 grams plain flour
- 250 ml milk
- 200 ml water
- 4 tablespoons caster sugar
- 60 grams melted butter
- A pinch of salt

### Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Then fry them, adding a little **lata** (likely butter or oil) for the first time
3. Cook one at a time

## Panettone

### Ingredients
- 7 eggs
- 1½ cups sugar
- 1 cup oil
- Zest of 1 lemon and 1 orange
- Self-raising flour (as much as it takes)

### Instructions
1. Beat the egg whites with the sugar
2. Add the yolks
3. Add the flour (about 2 cups total)
4. You can add **pan con forno** (likely raisins or candied fruit)
5. Bake at 320°F for half an hour to 35 minutes
