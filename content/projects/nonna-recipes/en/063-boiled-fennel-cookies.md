# Boiled Fennel Cookies

## Ingredients
- 1½ kilograms plain flour
- 1 sachet yeast
- 1½ tablespoons salt
- 1 glass oil
- 1 glass white wine
- Fennel seeds (to taste)

## Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients very well
2. They must be boiled first before baking
3. As they rise to the top, immediately take them out
4. Place on a towel
5. When they dry a little, bake them
