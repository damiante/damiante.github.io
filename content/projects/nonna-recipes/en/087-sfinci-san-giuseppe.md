# Sfinci di S. Giuseppe / St. Joseph's Cream Puffs

## Ingredients

### For the Pastry
- 275 ml water
- 125 grams butter
- A pinch of salt
- 150 grams flour, sifted
- 4 eggs
- 1 litre oil for frying

### For the Filling
- 500 grams ricotta (or more)
- 100 grams caster sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 4 tablespoons citrus peel, finely chopped
- 4 tablespoons chocolate buttons
- 2-3 tablespoons liqueur, to taste

## Instructions

### For the Pastry
1. Put the water in a pot over low heat
2. Add the butter and salt, melt and bring to a boil
3. Add all the flour at once
4. Lower the heat and stir vigorously until it forms a cream-like consistency
5. Transfer to a bowl to cool slightly
6. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition
7. Don't add the last egg if the dough becomes too soft
8. Heat oil to 360°F (180°C)
9. Use two spoons to form the puffs and drop them into the hot oil
10. Fry until golden and puffed
11. Drain on paper towels

### For the Filling
1. Mix ricotta with sugar, vanilla, finely chopped citrus peel, chocolate buttons, and liqueur
2. Mix well until smooth
3. Fill the cooled sfinci with the ricotta mixture
