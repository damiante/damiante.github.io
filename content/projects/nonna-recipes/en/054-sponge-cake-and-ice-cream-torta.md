# Sponge Cake and Ice Cream Torta

## Sponge Cake Base

### Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- ½ cup caster sugar
- ½ cup cornflour
- ½ teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 2 teaspoons plain flour

### Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Bake at 320°F for 45 minutes

## Ice Cream Torta (Torta di Gelato)

### Ingredients
- 1 sponge cake (as above), cut into 2 layers
- 9 tablespoons brandy
- 2 tablespoons powdered coffee
- 2 tablespoons cocoa/chocolate
- 1 cup ground almonds
- 1 packet red and green cherries
- 2 liters vanilla ice cream

### Instructions
1. Place a little ice cream in the dish
2. Add the sponge layer
3. Soak it with coffee and brandy
4. Add cherries and almonds
5. Continue layering until everything is finished
6. Freeze until ready to serve
