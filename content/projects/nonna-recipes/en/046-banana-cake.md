# Banana Cake

## Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 mashed bananas
- 1 cup sugar
- 1½ cups self-raising flour
- 4 eggs
- 2 tablespoons milk

## Instructions
1. Beat butter and sugar together
2. Add the eggs one at a time
3. Add the mashed bananas
4. Add the flour
5. Add the milk
6. Mix everything together
7. Bake at 350°F for 35 to 40 minutes
