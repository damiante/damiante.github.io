# Pizzelle Ripiena di Ricotta / Ricotta-Filled Cookies

## Ingredients

### For the Pastry
- 4 cups self-raising flour
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 5 tablespoons sour cream
- 125 grams unsalted butter
- 3 eggs

### For the Filling
- 400 grams ricotta
- Almond essence or vanilla
- 1 egg

## Instructions

1. Mix flour, sugar, sour cream, butter, and eggs to make the pastry
2. Knead well
3. Let rest for 30 minutes
4. Roll out using a pasta machine to thickness number 4
5. For the filling, mix ricotta with almond essence or vanilla and 1 egg
6. Cut the pastry into circles or rectangles
7. Fill with the ricotta mixture
8. Fold or close the pastry around the filling
9. Bake at 320째F (160째C) until golden
