# Almond Cookies (Two Versions)

## Version 1: **Biscm di Bicina**

### Ingredients
- 700 grams almonds, toasted and ground with skin on
- Zest of 2 lemons, grated
- 2 cups caster sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon stick
- 2 teaspoons cocoa
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

### Instructions
1. Cut the almonds with a knife into small pieces before baking
2. Peel them and cut into 3 or 4 pieces
3. Mix with egg whites and all the rest
4. Bake at 150°C

## Version 2: Almond Cookies

### Ingredients
- ¾ cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 3 cups **consal to** flour (self-raising?)

### Instructions
1. Beat sugar and butter
2. Add eggs one at a time
3. Add flour
4. Dip in egg white
5. Roll in almonds
6. Bake at 140°F for 15-18 minutes
