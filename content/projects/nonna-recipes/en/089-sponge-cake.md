# Pane di Spagna / Sponge Cake (Two Versions)

## Version 1: Classic Sponge Cake

### Ingredients
- 5 whole eggs plus 2 egg yolks
- 140 grams sugar
- 70 grams self-raising flour
- 70 grams cornflour
- Grated zest of 1 lemon
- A little vanilla

### Instructions
1. Beat eggs and sugar for 20 minutes until very thick and pale
2. Add grated lemon zest and vanilla
3. Gently fold in the self-raising flour and cornflour
4. Pour into a greased and lined pan
5. Bake at 170째C for 35 minutes

## Version 2: Simple Sponge Cake

### Ingredients
- 3 whole eggs
- 105 grams sugar
- 120 grams self-raising flour

### Instructions
1. Beat eggs and sugar together very well
2. Gently fold in the flour
3. Pour into a greased pan
4. Bake at 180째C for 20 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 180째C
