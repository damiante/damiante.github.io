# Cannelloni di Carne / Meat Cannelloni (Two Recipes)

## Recipe 1: Rolled Meat Cannelloni

### Ingredients
- 1 slice of meat, pounded thin with a mallet
- A little ground meat
- 1 egg
- A little mozzarella

### Instructions
1. Pound the meat slice thin with a mallet
2. Mix the ground meat with an egg and a little mozzarella
3. Spread the mixture on the meat slice
4. Roll it up tightly
5. Fry lightly in a pan
6. Transfer to the oven to finish cooking
7. Serve with vegetables

## Recipe 2: Cannelloni Ripieni (Filled Cannelloni Tubes)

### Ingredients
- Cannelloni pasta tubes
- Ground meat
- Tomato sauce

### Instructions
1. Prepare the ground meat as you would for meatballs (polpette)
2. Fry the meat lightly before filling the cannelloni
3. Fill the cannelloni tubes with the cooked meat
4. Place in a baking dish
5. Cover with tomato sauce
6. Bake until heated through and bubbly
