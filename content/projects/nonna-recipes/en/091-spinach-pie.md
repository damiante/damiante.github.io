# Torta di Spinaci / Spinach Pie

## Ingredients

### For the Filling
- Spinach, boiled without water
- Soft cheese (approximately 2-3 varieties)
- 1 egg

### For the Pastry
- Approximately 1 kilogram flour in total
- 6 eggs
- A little oil

## Instructions

1. Boil the spinach without adding water (use only the water that clings to the leaves)
2. Drain and mix the spinach with soft cheese and 1 egg
3. Make the pastry with the flour and 6 eggs, adding a little oil
4. Knead the dough well
5. Roll out the pastry and place it in a greased dish
6. Fill with the spinach and cheese mixture
7. Make strips of pastry and arrange them on top in a lattice pattern
8. Brush the top with a little oil
9. Bake at 350째F (175째C) for 40 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 350째F (175째C)

**Note:** You can use 2 or 3 different types of cheese for more flavor.
