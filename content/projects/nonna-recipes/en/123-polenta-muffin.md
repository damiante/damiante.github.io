# Polenta Muffin / Savory Sweet Potato Muffins

## Ingredients

- 3-4 spring onions, fresh
- 1 egg
- ½ cup oil
- ⅔ cup milk
- ½ sweet potato, grated
- ⅔ cup grated cheese
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 cup wholemeal self-raising flour
- 1 cup white self-raising flour

## Instructions

1. Mix egg, oil, and milk together
2. Add the grated sweet potato, grated cheese, chopped spring onions, and cayenne pepper
3. Add both flours (wholemeal and white)
4. Mix well
5. Fill muffin tins
6. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
