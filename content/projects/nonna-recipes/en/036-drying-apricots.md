# Drying Apricots

## Ingredients
- 200 grams **burca** preservative (likely sulphite or similar wine/fruit preservative)
- 10 liters of water
- Fresh apricots

## Instructions
1. Dissolve 200 grams of **burca** preservative in 10 liters of water
2. Soak the apricots for 12 hours
3. Remove and dry the apricots
4. This is the same substance used in wine making for **bisnni** (barrels?)

## Notes
This appears to be a traditional method for preserving apricots using a sulphite solution before drying.
