# Orange Cake with Icing / Torta di Arancio con Glassa

## Ingredients

### For the Cake
- 1 medium orange
- 200 grams butter, melted
- 3 eggs, lightly beaten
- 1 cup sugar
- 1½ cups self-raising flour

### For the Icing
- 140 ml lemon juice
- 1 small orange
- 1 cup icing sugar
- 1 teaspoon boiling water

## Instructions

### For the Cake
1. Grind the orange with the peel in a food processor
2. Add the melted butter
3. Add the eggs
4. Add the sugar
5. Finally add the flour
6. Mix well
7. Pour into paper cases or a greased dish like making scones
8. Bake at 180°C for 10 minutes (if in small cases)

### For the Icing
1. Mix lemon juice, juice from the small orange, icing sugar, and boiling water
2. Pour over the warm cakes

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
