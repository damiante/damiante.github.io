# Bottling Sultanas

## Ingredients
- 3 cups water
- 1 cup sugar
- Sultanas (quantity not specified)

## Instructions
1. Dissolve the sugar in the water
2. Fill the bottles with sultanas
3. Pour the sugar syrup over the sultanas
4. Close the bottles tightly
5. Boil the bottles for 3 minutes
6. Store
