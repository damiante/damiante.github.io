# Strudel

## Ingredients

### For the Pastry
- 1 egg
- 50 grams butter
- 50 grams sugar
- 400 grams flour
- 1 teaspoon yeast

### For the Filling
- 1 kilogram apples, cut into small pieces and dried in a pan
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- Sultanas (raisins)
- Pine nuts
- Butter, melted

## Instructions

1. Mix egg, butter, sugar, flour, and yeast together to make the pastry dough
2. Roll out the dough very thin
3. Brush with melted butter
4. Cut the apples into small pieces and dry them in a pan (cook until moisture is reduced)
5. Mix the dried apples with sugar, sultanas, and pine nuts
6. Spread the apple filling over the pastry
7. Roll up the strudel
8. Brush the top with beaten egg
9. Bake at 180°C for approximately 40 minutes until golden

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
