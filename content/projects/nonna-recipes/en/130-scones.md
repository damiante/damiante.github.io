# Scongi / Scones

## Ingredients

- 4 cups self-raising flour
- 1 bottle (300g) cream
- 1 bottle milk (same size as the cream bottle)

## Instructions

1. Mix flour, cream, and milk together
2. Knead until just combined (do not overmix)
3. Form into scone shapes
4. Bake at 380째F (190째C) for 15 minutes
