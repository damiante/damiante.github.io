# Tiramisu with Ladyfingers

## Ingredients
- One layer of ladyfinger cookies (savoiardi)
- Coffee for soaking
- Cream (see separate cream recipe)
- A bit of **cutunrozzo** (unclear dialect word)

## Instructions
1. Soak the ladyfinger cookies in coffee
2. Place one layer of cookies
3. Then one layer of cream
4. Then cookies **sbrusba** (probably crumbled or other dialect term)
5. Then soak them in coffee
6. Then the cream
7. Then a bit of **cutunrozzo**
8. Then put it in the freezer

**NOTE:** Dessert to eat later.
