# Torta Rotonda (Round Cake)

## Ingredients
- 1½ cups self-raising flour
- 20 grams butter
- 2 tablespoons caster sugar
- Milk (for mixing and brushing)

### Filling
- Nutella
- Ground walnuts
- Ground almonds

## Instructions
1. Mix butter and flour, then beat
2. Add milk to form a dough
3. Roll out like lasagna (thin)
4. Spread a little Nutella with a knife
5. Add ground walnuts and almonds
6. Roll it up round
7. **Per attico** (to stick/seal?)
8. Brush the top with milk
9. Place in the oven
10. The next day, bake again
