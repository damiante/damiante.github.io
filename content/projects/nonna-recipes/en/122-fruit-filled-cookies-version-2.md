# Biscotti Ripiena con Frutta / Fruit-Filled Cookies (Version 2)

## Ingredients

### For the Pastry
- 500 grams self-raising flour
- 200 grams sugar
- 200 grams butter, melted
- 1 packet vanilla
- 2 eggs
- Grated lemon zest

### For the Filling
- 200 grams clean almonds or dried figs, chopped
- 160 grams sugar
- A little cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Mix flour, sugar, melted butter, vanilla, eggs, and lemon zest to make the pastry
2. Mix almonds or figs with sugar and cinnamon for the filling
3. Roll out the pastry with a rolling pin
4. Form into ravioli shapes or long rolls
5. Brush with a little milk on top
6. Sprinkle with a little sugar
7. Bake at 200°C for 15 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 200°C
