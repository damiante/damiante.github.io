# Torta di Mandorle / Almond Tart

## Ingredients

### For the Cookie Dough Base
- Use the same dough as for biscotti (butter, flour, sugar, eggs)

### For the Filling
- Use the same mixture as for amaretti (ground almonds, sugar, egg whites)

### For the Topping
- Sugar (icing sugar or granulated)

## Instructions

1. Prepare the cookie dough as you would for biscotti
2. Roll out the dough on parchment paper
3. Place the dough on a baking sheet with parchment paper underneath
4. Spread the amaretti mixture (almond paste) over the dough base
5. Make strips of the cookie dough and arrange them on top in a lattice pattern
6. Sprinkle a little sugar on top
7. Bake at 320째F (160째C) for 40 minutes
