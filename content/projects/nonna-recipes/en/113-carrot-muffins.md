# Biscotti nelle Carte Rotonda / Carrot Muffins

## Ingredients

- 200 grams carrots, grated
- 300 grams flour (plain or self-raising with baking powder)
- 100 grams sugar
- 100 ml oil
- 50 grams almond flour
- 2 eggs
- 1 packet baking powder
- 1 pinch salt
- Grated lemon zest

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Fill muffin cups or paper cases
3. Bake at 180°C for 15 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
