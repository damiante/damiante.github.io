# Taralli di Coppola / Fennel Taralli

## Ingredients

- 1 kilogram plain flour
- 1 glass seed oil
- ½ tablespoon salt
- 25 grams yeast
- Fennel seeds, to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients together to form a dough
2. Roll out the dough using a pasta machine to desired thickness
3. Cut into strips and form into rings
4. Bring a large pot of water to a boil
5. Drop the taralli into the boiling water
6. When they float to the surface, remove them immediately with a slotted spoon
7. Place the boiled taralli on a baking sheet
8. Bake until golden and crispy
