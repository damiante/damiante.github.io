# Italian Cookies and Fennel Cookies

## Fennel Cookies

### Ingredients
- 6 eggs
- 1 cup self-raising flour (rest plain)
- ½ glass oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ glass sugar
- 1 glass vermouth or grappa
- A little fennel seeds

### Instructions
1. Beat eggs
2. Add all ingredients
3. Roll through pasta machine to number 2
4. Stack one on top of the other
5. After half an hour, roll with a rolling pin

## Italian Cookies (Biscotti Italiani)

### Ingredients
- 1 pound plain flour
- ½ pound butter (225 grams)
- ½ pound sugar
- 1 teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- ½ cup milk

### Instructions
1. Mix everything well
2. Shape as you like
3. Bake at 375°F
