# Paste di Mandorle Buone (Angela's Version) - PARTIAL

## Ingredients
- 1 kilogram and 100 grams ground almonds
- 750 grams caster sugar
- Almond essence
- Vanilla essence
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 8 eggs (use 6 first, if too hard add the other 2)
- Cornflour (for dusting)

## Instructions
1. Mix everything together with a fork
2. Use 6 eggs first
3. If it comes out too hard, add the other 2 eggs
4. **Grasti** (roll/dust) in cornflour
5. Bake at 180°C

## Smaller Version
- ½ kilogram almonds
- 250 grams caster sugar
- ½ tablespoon honey
- Vanilla essence
- Maybe around 3-4 eggs
- 50 grams cornflour or similar
