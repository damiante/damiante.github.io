# Pavlova

## Ingredients
- 4 egg whites
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 cup sugar
- A pinch of salt
- 1 tablespoon cornflour
- Essence (vanilla or almond)

## Instructions
1. Beat the egg whites with salt until stiff peaks form (like snow)
2. Add the sugar and beat again
3. Add the vinegar, cornflour, and essence
4. Mix everything together
5. Shape into a pavlova base
6. Bake at 300°F for 10 minutes
7. Then reduce temperature to 200°F and continue baking
