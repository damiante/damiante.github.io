# Checco

## Ingredients
- ½ kilogram self-raising flour
- ½ kilogram sugar
- 5 eggs
- 250 grams fresh ricotta
- A few cherries
- A little chocolate
- A little vanilla
- Some almonds
- Grated lemon zest
- 3 tablespoons **casted pover** (caster powder/baking powder)
- 3 tablespoons water

## Instructions
1. Beat the eggs with the ground sugar
2. Add all other ingredients together and mix well
3. Pour into baking pan
4. Bake at 375°F for half an hour
5. Let cool in the pan with a cloth cover
