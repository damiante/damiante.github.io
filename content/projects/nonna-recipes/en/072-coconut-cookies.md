# Cocco Bello Biscotti / Coconut Cookies

## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup oil
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 cups flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 cup coconut (desiccated)
- ½ cup milk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla

## Instructions

1. Beat eggs and oil together
2. Add sugar and beat well
3. Mix in flour, baking powder, and coconut
4. Add milk and vanilla
5. Mix everything together gradually, adding flour a little at a time
6. Bake at 187°C until golden

**Oven Temperature:** 187°C
