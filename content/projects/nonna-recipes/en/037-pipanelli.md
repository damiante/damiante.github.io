# Pipanelli

## Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- A little essence (vanilla or almond)
- 1 cup sugar
- 250 grams almonds
- 2 cups plain flour

## Instructions
1. Beat the eggs, essence, and sugar together
2. Add the almonds and walnuts
3. Add flour
4. Shape into logs
5. Bake until they take on color
6. After one day, slice into thin slices
7. Bake again (double-baked like biscotti)
