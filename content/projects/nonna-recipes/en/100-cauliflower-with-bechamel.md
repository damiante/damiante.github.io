# Cavolfiore con Besciamela / Cauliflower with Béchamel

## Ingredients

- 1 cauliflower
- 200 ml milk
- 50 grams butter
- 50 grams flour
- A little soft cheese
- Breadcrumbs
- Grated cheese

## Instructions

1. Boil the cauliflower until half-cooked
2. Drain and place in a round baking dish
3. Make the béchamel sauce: melt 50g butter, add 50g flour, then gradually add 200ml milk, stirring constantly
4. Add a little soft cheese to the béchamel and mix well
5. Pour the béchamel sauce over the cauliflower
6. Sprinkle breadcrumbs and grated cheese on top
7. Drizzle a little melted butter over the top
8. Bake at 360°F (180°C) for approximately 30 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 360°F (180°C)
