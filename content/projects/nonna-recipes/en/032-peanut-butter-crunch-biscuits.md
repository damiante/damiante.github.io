# Peanut Butter Crunch Biscuits

## Ingredients
- 250 grams margarine
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tablespoon golden syrup
- 1 tablespoon bicarbonate of soda
- 4 eggs
- 2 cups quick oats
- 2 cups coconut
- 2 cups self-raising flour
- 4 small packets peanuts, ground

## Instructions
1. Beat together margarine, sugar, golden syrup, and bicarbonate of soda
2. Add eggs
3. Add quick oats, coconut, self-raising flour, and ground peanuts
4. Mix well
5. Roll into small balls
6. Flatten with a fork
7. Let them cool and harden before removing from baking sheet
8. Bake at 350Â°F
