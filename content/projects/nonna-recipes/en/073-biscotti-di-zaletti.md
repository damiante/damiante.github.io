# Biscotti di Zaletti

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 kilogram flour (half plain, half self-raising)
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 tablespoon salt
- ¼ cup oil
- 1 tablespoon grappa or liqueur
- 3 teaspoons fennel seeds

## Instructions

1. Beat the eggs with all the other ingredients
2. Mix everything together well
3. Place the dough in the refrigerator for 30 to 45 minutes
4. Roll out the dough using a pasta machine to desired thickness
5. Shape as desired
6. Bake at 200°C until golden

**Oven Temperature:** 200°C
