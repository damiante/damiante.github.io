# Peperoni o Giardiniera / Pickled Peppers and Vegetables

## Ingredients

- 2 litres white vinegar
- 1 litre water
- ½ cup sugar
- A handful of salt
- Peppers, cauliflower, carrots, small cucumbers, or other vegetables

## Method

1. Bring the vinegar to a boil in a large pot
2. When boiling, add the peppers or vegetables (cauliflower, carrots, small cucumbers)
3. Boil for 1 minute
4. Prepare clean, hot jars
5. Fill the jars with the vegetables and hot pickling liquid
6. Close the jars tightly with lids
7. Turn the jars upside down while still hot to seal
