# Vegetable Tower (**Patate Lunghi Tapnieri**)

## Ingredients
- Potatoes (long **tapnieri**)
- 1 slice tomato per layer
- 1 slice zucchini per layer
- 1 slice eggplant per layer
- A little oil
- Cheese slices

## Instructions
1. After baking the potatoes
2. Layer with:
   - 1 slice tomato
   - 1 slice zucchini
   - 1 slice eggplant (**milanzane**)
3. **Taffieri fino** (stack until done)
4. Drizzle a little oil on top
5. Bake at 200Â°C for 1 hour
6. Then add a slice of cheese on top
7. Bake for another 5 minutes
