# Biscotti al Finocchio / Fennel Cookies (Version 3)

## Ingredients

- 500 grams self-raising flour
- 100 grams unsalted butter
- 1 glass white wine
- 3 teaspoons fennel seeds
- 2 eggs

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter and let cool
2. Mix the butter with the flour using your hands
3. Add eggs, white wine, and fennel seeds
4. Knead the dough well
5. Place in the refrigerator in a plastic bag for 30-45 minutes
6. Roll out the dough using a pasta machine to thickness number 3
7. Roll up like macaroni (form into cylinders)
8. Close/seal with a spoon where one overlaps the other
9. Bake until golden
