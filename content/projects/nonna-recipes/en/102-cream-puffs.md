# Cream Puffs / Crema Paffs

## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 125 grams butter
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup self-raising flour

## Instructions

1. Put the butter in a pot and melt
2. When melted, add the water
3. When it boils, add all the flour at once
4. Stir vigorously until it forms a dough and pulls away from the sides
5. Remove from heat and let cool
6. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition
7. Using a spoon, drop spoonfuls of dough onto greased baking sheets
8. Makes 22-23 puffs
9. Bake at 250°C for 1 hour

**Oven Temperature:** 250°C

**Note:** These can be filled with sweet cream, custard, or ricotta filling.
