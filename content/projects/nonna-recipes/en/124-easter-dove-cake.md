# Colomba di Pasqua / Easter Dove Cake

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 100 grams sugar
- 100 grams soft butter
- 350 grams flour
- 1 packet yeast for sweets
- 1 glass milk
- 1 pinch salt
- 110 grams candied orange

### For the Topping
- Ground almonds mixed with egg white
- Sliced almonds
- Granulated sugar

## Method

1. Beat the eggs separately (whites and yolks)
2. Mix everything together except the egg whites
3. Add the egg whites last after everything is mixed
4. Pour into a greased dove-shaped pan or regular pan
5. Bake at 160°C for 30-40 minutes
6. When half-cooked, spread a mixture of ground almonds mixed with egg white on top
7. Sprinkle with sliced almonds and granulated sugar
8. Continue baking until done

**Oven Temperature:** 160°C
