# Coscia di Gallo o di Tachino / Chicken or Turkey Thigh with Zucchini

## Ingredients

- Chicken or turkey thighs
- Zucchini, sliced
- Eggplant, sliced into rounds
- Mozzarella
- Oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Slice the zucchini and grill them
2. Slice the eggplant into rounds and fry them in a pan
3. Season the chicken or turkey thighs and fry them lightly in a pan
4. Transfer the meat to an oven-safe dish
5. Place a slice of grilled zucchini on top of each piece of meat
6. Add a little mozzarella on top
7. Bake until the meat is cooked through and the cheese is melted and golden
