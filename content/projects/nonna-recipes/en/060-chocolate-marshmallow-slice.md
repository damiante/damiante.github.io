# Chocolate Marshmallow Slice

## Ingredients
- 2 packets marshmallows (approximately 250 grams each, total 450 grams)
- 1½ packets white chocolate or regular chocolate
- 1 cup toasted almonds
- 2 packages **lira wafers**

## Instructions
1. Cook for 20 minutes or until it comes away from the pot
2. Place a little weight on top
3. Put in the refrigerator
4. Cut when cold
