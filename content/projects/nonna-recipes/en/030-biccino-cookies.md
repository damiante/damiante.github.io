# Biccino Cookies

## Ingredients
- 2 eggs
- 1 pound unsalted butter
- 1 cup raisins
- Self-raising flour (as much as it takes, but don't make it too hard)
- A little stick cinnamon, soaked in vanilla
- A little sugar
- **Cirnellin** (cinnamon?)

## Instructions
1. Mix eggs and butter together
2. Add raisins and flour (don't make the dough too hard)
3. Soak a cinnamon stick in vanilla
4. Make a hole in the center of each cookie
5. Place the soaked cinnamon stick in the hole
6. Sprinkle a little sugar and **cirnellin** in the middle
7. Bake until done
