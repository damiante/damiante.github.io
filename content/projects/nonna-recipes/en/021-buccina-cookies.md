# Buccina Cookies

## Ingredients
- 8 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- ½ pound butter
- A little essence (vanilla or almond)
- Flour (as much as it takes)
- **Arisciuga** (raisins) for topping
- Almonds for topping

## Instructions
1. Beat together eggs, sugar, butter, and essence
2. Add flour gradually until the dough comes together
3. Shape the cookies
4. Top with **arisciuga** (raisins) and almonds
5. Bake until done

## Notes
This recipe also includes instructions for another cookie variation ("Biscotti a S."):
- 2 pounds plain flour
- ½ pound lard
- A little **cest** (zest)
- 1 cup sugar
- A pinch of salt
- After shaping, add chocolate topping
