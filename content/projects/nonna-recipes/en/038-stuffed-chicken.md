# Stuffed Chicken

## Ingredients
### For the Stuffing
- 2 boiled eggs
- 200 grams ground meat
- 300 grams **bpsaide** (likely sausage or similar)
- 250 grams prosciutto
- 200 grams cheese (any quality)

### For Cooking
- 1 liter white wine
- A little water
- 1 stalk celery
- 1 onion
- 1 carrot

## Instructions
1. Prepare the chicken
2. Mix together all the stuffing ingredients
3. Stuff the chicken
4. Tie it closed
5. Place in a pot with white wine, water, celery, onion, and carrot
6. Cook until done
7. Let cool
8. **Stetla tagligenti e setta** (slice thinly and serve) when cold

## Alternative Method
You can also roast or boil the chicken instead of braising.
