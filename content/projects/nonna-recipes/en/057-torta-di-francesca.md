# Torta di Francesca

## Ingredients
- ½ kilogram flour
- 300 grams sugar
- 200 grams butter
- 1 sachet vanilla
- 5 eggs
- Milk (as much as it takes)
- 100 grams cocoa

## Instructions
1. Mix with milk (as much as it takes)
2. Divide batter: make some white and some with cocoa
3. Put in the dish alternating white and cocoa in a **smischiato** (mixed/marbled) pattern of two colors
4. Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes, then reduce to 300°F
5. Or bake at 160°C
6. **Mettere farina e mettere con flour** (dust with flour)
