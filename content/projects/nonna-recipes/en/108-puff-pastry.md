# Pasta Sfoglia / Puff Pastry

## Ingredients

- 250 grams butter
- 250 grams flour
- 1 pinch of salt
- 1 glass water
- 3 tablespoons sugar

## Instructions

1. Mix flour, butter (reserve some for folding), salt, water, and sugar
2. Knead into a smooth dough
3. Roll out the dough
4. Fold and roll several times with butter between layers to create flaky layers
5. Refrigerate between folds
6. Use for various sweet or savory recipes

**Note:** This is a basic puff pastry recipe that can be used for multiple preparations.
