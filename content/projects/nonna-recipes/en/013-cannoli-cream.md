# Cannoli Cream

## Ingredients
- 4 tablespoons plain flour
- 6 eggs
- 1 tablespoon sugar per egg (= 6 tablespoons total)
- 1 liter milk
- Essence (vanilla or almond)

## Yield notes:
- 8 liters of milk fills 15 cannoli
- 11 cups of milk fills 19 cannoli

## Instructions
1. Mix the plain flour with the eggs
2. Add 1 tablespoon of sugar for each egg
3. Add the milk
4. Add the essence
5. Cook while stirring until it thickens
