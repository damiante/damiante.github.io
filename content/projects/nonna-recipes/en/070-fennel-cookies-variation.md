# Biscotti col Finocchio (Variation)

## Ingredients
- 2 cups plain flour + self-raising flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup caster sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- 3 teaspoons finely chopped lemon peel
- 1 teaspoon fennel seed
- ½ cup toasted chopped almonds

## Instructions
1. Beat eggs
2. Mix all ingredients together
3. Shape into logs
4. Bake at 180°C for 35 minutes
5. Take out and slice them
6. Cook again for another 25 minutes
7. They should be a little hard

## For Lasagna (Additional Note)
- Beat 6 eggs and sift
- Can add a little water
- Roll through pasta machine to number 5 setting
