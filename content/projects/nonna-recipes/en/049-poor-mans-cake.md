# La Torta dei Poveri (Poor Man's Cake)

## Ingredients
- 300 grams stale bread
- 6 amaretti cookies
- 1 liter milk
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons pine nuts
- 3 tablespoons sultanas
- 2 tablespoons cocoa
- 6 tablespoons sugar
- 50 grams butter, melted
- 2 small glasses of liqueur

## Instructions
1. Crumble the bread and soak it in the milk
2. Soak the amaretti cookies in the liqueur
3. After half an hour, mix the soaked cookies into the bread mixture
4. Beat the eggs and mix in
5. Add the pine nuts, sultanas, cocoa, and melted butter
6. Mix everything together
7. Bake at 180Â°C in the center of the oven for about 1 hour
