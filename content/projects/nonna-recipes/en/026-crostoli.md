# Crostoli

## Ingredients
- 5 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar per egg (10 tablespoons total)
- 2 ounces butter
- ½ glass sweet sherry
- Flour: half plain, half self-raising
- 1 glass sweet sherry

## Instructions
1. Beat eggs with sugar
2. Add butter and sweet sherry
3. Mix in the flour (half plain, half self-raising)
4. Roll the dough through a pasta machine to number setting (not specified in transcription)
5. Cut and shape
6. Fry until golden
7. Dust with powdered sugar
