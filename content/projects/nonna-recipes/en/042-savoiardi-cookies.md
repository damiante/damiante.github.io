# Savoiardi Cookies

## Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- ½ pound margarine
- 1 cup sugar
- Self-raising flour (as much as it takes)
- A little lemon essence
- 2 vanilla beans

## Instructions
1. Mix everything together well
2. Shape into long fingers or small balls
3. Bake at 250°F
4. Make them small
