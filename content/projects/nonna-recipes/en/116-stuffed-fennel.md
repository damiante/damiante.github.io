# Finochi Ripieni / Stuffed Fennel

## Ingredients

- 2-3 fennel bulbs
- Sun-dried tomatoes, cut into small pieces
- Black olives (optional)
- Mozzarella, cut into small pieces
- Breadcrumbs

## Instructions

1. Boil the fennel bulbs until tender
2. Drain and let cool
3. Cut out the inside of the fennel and chop into small pieces
4. Mix the chopped fennel with sun-dried tomatoes, black olives (optional), and mozzarella cut into small pieces
5. Place the fennel bulbs in a baking dish
6. Fill them with the mixture
7. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top
8. Bake at 170°C for 15 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 170°C
