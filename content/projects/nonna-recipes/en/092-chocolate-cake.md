# Dolce / Chocolate Cake

## Ingredients

- 1 litre eggs (approximately 12-15 eggs)
- 60 grams flour
- 60 grams butter, melted in a double boiler
- 40 grams cocoa powder
- 60 grams chocolate, melted in a double boiler
- 50 grams sugar
- Dry sultanas (optional)

## Instructions

1. Beat the eggs with the sugar
2. Add melted butter, flour, cocoa powder, and melted chocolate
3. Mix everything together well
4. Add dry sultanas if desired
5. Pour into a greased pan
6. Bake for 18 minutes

**Note:** Baking time is short - watch carefully to avoid overbaking.
