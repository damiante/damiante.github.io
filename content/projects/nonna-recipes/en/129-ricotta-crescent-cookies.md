# Biscotti a Cornetto / Ricotta Crescent Cookies

## Ingredients

- 250 grams flour
- 130 grams ricotta
- 1 egg
- A little water

### For the Filling
- Nutella or jam

## Instructions

1. Mix flour, ricotta, egg, and a little water to make a dough
2. Form like Gina's cookies (crescents)
3. Fill with Nutella or jam
4. Bake until golden
