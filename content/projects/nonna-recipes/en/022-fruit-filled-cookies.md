# Fruit-Filled Cookies

## Ingredients
### Dough
- 2 parts self-raising flour
- 1 part plain flour
- 6 eggs
- 2 tablespoons sugar per egg (12 tablespoons total)
- 1 cup sweet sherry
- 1 cup oil

### Filling
- Fruit filling of your choice

## Instructions
1. Mix all dough ingredients together
2. Roll the dough through a pasta machine to number 2 setting
3. Fill with fruit filling
4. Bake until done
