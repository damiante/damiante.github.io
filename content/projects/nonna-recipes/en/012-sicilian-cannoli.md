# Sicilian Cannoli

## Ingredients

### For the cannoli shells:
- 2 pounds plain flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 cups (or 2 cups) sweet sherry (or other liqueur)
- 4 ounces (or 2 ounces) butter
- 3 eggs (or 6 eggs) whole
- And 3 yolks and whites
- 2 extra whites for sealing them

### Yield notes:
- 11 cups of milk fills 70 cannoli
- 3Â½ cups of milk fills 13 cannoli
- 11 cups of milk fills 19 cannoli
- Without (some ingredient) makes 100 cannoli
- With butter cream, 4 cups of milk fills 13-14 cannoli (16-17)

## Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients
2. Roll them with the pasta machine at number 4
3. Let rest
4. Use the whites to seal them
5. Fry in oil

### For the filling (separate recipe):
See cannoli cream recipe
