# Apple Pie

## Ingredients
- 4 ounces butter
- ½ cup sugar
- 2 cups **congala** flour (self-raising flour?)
- 1 egg
- ¼ cup milk
- Green apples (quantity not specified)

## Instructions
1. Mix butter, sugar, flour, egg, and milk together
2. Peel and prepare the green apples
3. **Ruspirla** (roll out?) to small pieces
4. Place in pie dish
5. Bake until golden brown
6. When it colors, it's done
