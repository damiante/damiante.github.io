# Ceppette di Pasta Frolla / Honey Almond Tarts

## Ingredients

### For the Pastry
- 200 grams butter
- 100 grams sugar
- 300 grams flour
- 3 egg yolks
- A little liqueur or Marsala

### For the Filling and Topping
- Honey
- 1 egg yolk (for brushing)
- Chopped almonds

## Instructions

1. Make the pasta frolla by mixing butter, sugar, flour, egg yolks, and a little liqueur or Marsala
2. Roll out the pastry and line small tart molds
3. Fill each tart with honey
4. Brush the top edges with beaten egg yolk
5. Sprinkle chopped almonds on top
6. Bake until golden
