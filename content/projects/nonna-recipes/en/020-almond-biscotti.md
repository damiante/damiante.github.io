# Almond Biscotti

## Ingredients
- 4 cups flour
- 1½ cups sugar
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¾ cup oil
- 4 large eggs
- 2 teaspoons almond extract
- 1 cup sliced almonds

## Instructions
1. Mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
2. In a third bowl beat together oil, eggs, and extract
3. Add dry to liquid until well mixed
4. Add the nuts - mix
5. The dough will be sticky
6. Divide into 3 portions
7. Roll into logs about 2" in diameter
8. Place on a greased baking sheet or with parchment paper
9. Flatten slightly
10. Bake at 350°F for 35 minutes or until golden
11. Cool completely on a rack
12. Slice with a sharp serrated knife
13. Then cut at an oblique angle into ½ inch slices
14. Place the cut pieces on a baking sheet with parchment paper
15. Bake at 325°F for about 10 minutes
