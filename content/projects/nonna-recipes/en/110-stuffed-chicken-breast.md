# Petto di Gallo Ripieno / Stuffed Chicken Breast

## Ingredients

- Chicken breast
- Mushrooms
- Soft cheese
- A little oil

## Instructions

1. Cut the chicken breast open to butterfly it (cut it wide)
2. Fill the middle with mushrooms, soft cheese, and any other desired fillings
3. Close the chicken breast around the filling
4. Cook in a pan with a little oil until browned
5. Transfer to the oven to finish cooking until the chicken is cooked through
