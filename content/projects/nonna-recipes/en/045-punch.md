# Punch

## Ingredients
- 1 kilogram pineapple
- 1 tube white wine
- 1 bottle purple soda
- Strawberries (in **dolzi** - sweet?)
- Apples
- Rockmelon, cut into pieces
- Orange juice (from **chisci di aranciate**)

## Instructions
1. Cut the pineapple, strawberries, apples, and rockmelon into pieces
2. Mix with white wine
3. Add the purple soda
4. Add orange juice
5. Serve cold

## Alternative Version
Without wine is better.
