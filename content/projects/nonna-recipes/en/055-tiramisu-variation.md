# Tiramisu (Variation)

## Ingredients
- 1 packet sponge finger biscuits
- Cocoa (for dusting)
- Coffee
- 300 ml cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 120 grams sugar
- 100 grams soft cream cheese
- 100 grams mascarpone cheese
- Dash vanilla essence
- Sambuca (to taste)

## Instructions
1. Mix all cream ingredients together (cream, egg yolks, sugar, cream cheese, mascarpone, vanilla)
2. Soak biscuits in coffee and place one layer on tray
3. Cover layer with Â½ of cream mixture
4. Place another layer of soaked biscuits
5. Cover with rest of cream
6. Dust top with cocoa
7. Leave for 24 hours before serving
