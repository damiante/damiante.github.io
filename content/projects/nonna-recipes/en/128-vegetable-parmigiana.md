# Parmigiana di Patate, Melanzane e Zucchini / Vegetable Parmigiana

## Ingredients

- 5 potatoes, boiled half-cooked
- 2 eggplants, sliced and fried in a pan
- 3 zucchini, sliced and fried in a pan
- Mozzarella
- Grated cheese

### For the Béchamel
- 30 grams butter
- 30 grams flour
- 300 ml milk

## Method

1. Make the béchamel: melt the butter, add the flour, then gradually add the milk, stirring constantly
2. In a baking dish, create layers:
   - Start with potatoes
   - Add a little béchamel
   - Layer of zucchini and béchamel
   - Layer of eggplant and béchamel
3. Add mozzarella and grated cheese to each layer
4. Bake at 300°F (150°C) for 30 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 300°F (150°C)
