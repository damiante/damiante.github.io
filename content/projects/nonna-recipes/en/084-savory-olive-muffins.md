# Muffin / Savory Olive Muffins

## Ingredients

- 180 grams self-raising flour
- 100 grams oil
- 120 grams milk
- 120 grams cream
- 3 eggs
- 100-150 grams black olives, chopped

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients together until well combined
2. Fill muffin tins with the batter
3. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes or until golden and cooked through

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
