# Torta di Frutta / Yeasted Fruit Cake

## Ingredients

- 400 grams flour
- 20 grams fresh yeast
- 1 glass milk
- 300 grams cream
- 10 grams sugar
- Dried fruit of all varieties (raisins, figs, candied cherries, etc.)

## Instructions

1. Dissolve the yeast in a little warm water with a little flour
2. Let it rise for approximately 45 minutes
3. Mix everything together: the risen yeast mixture, remaining flour, milk, cream, sugar, and dried fruit
4. Mix well
5. Let rise again until doubled in size
6. Place cherries and a little sugar on top
7. Bake at 180°C

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
