# Ricotta / Homemade Ricotta

## Ingredients

- 3 litres milk
- 270 ml water
- 220 ml white vinegar
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Dissolve the salt in the water
2. Heat the milk in a large pot
3. Just before the milk begins to boil, add the vinegar and water mixture
4. Stir gently - the milk will begin to curdle
5. Remove from heat and let stand for a few minutes
6. Strain through cheesecloth to separate the curds from the whey
7. Let the ricotta drain until desired consistency is reached
8. Use fresh in recipes
