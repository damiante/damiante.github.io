# Fennel Cookies

## Version 1 (Traditional)

### Ingredients
- 6 eggs
- ½ glass sugar
- ½ glass oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 glass white wine or vermouth
- A little fennel seeds
- ½ glass self-raising flour
- Remaining flour: plain

### Instructions
1. Beat eggs, salt, sugar, oil, and wine together
2. **Inboltuarli** (wrap/let rest) for 3 hours
3. Roll through pasta machine to number 1 setting
4. Let rest for 3 more hours
5. Shape and bake

## Version 2 (Simplified)

### Ingredients
- 6 eggs
- ½ glass oil
- ½ glass sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- A little grappa
- Fennel seeds
- Plain flour (as needed)

### Instructions
1. Beat sugar and eggs together
2. Add oil, salt, and grappa
3. Add plain flour
4. Roll through pasta machine to number 1 setting
5. Stack one on top of the other
6. After half an hour, stretch them long or round
7. You can also add a little self-raising flour
