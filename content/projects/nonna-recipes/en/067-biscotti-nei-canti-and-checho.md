# Biscotti nei Canti and Checho

## Biscotti nei Canti

### Ingredients
- 6 tablespoons **consal to** flour (self-raising?)
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 pinch of salt
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons milk

### Instructions
1. Beat sugar and butter
2. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each egg
3. Add flour and milk little by little
4. Last add the rest of the flour and salt

## Checho

### Ingredients
- 2 cups heaped **consal to** flour
- 1½ cups sugar
- 2 eggs
- **B.p g.** butter (baking powder and butter?)
- ½ cup milk
- 1 packet dried fruit
- A little vanilla

### Instructions
1. Beat sugar and butter
2. Add eggs one at a time
3. Add milk
4. Add dried fruit
5. Add flour
6. Bake (temperature not specified)
