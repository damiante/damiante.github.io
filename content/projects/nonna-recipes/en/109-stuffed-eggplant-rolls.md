# Melanzane Ripieni / Stuffed Eggplant Rolls

## Ingredients

- Eggplant slices
- Avocado slices
- Red pepper slices
- Flour
- Water
- Almonds, chopped or ground
- Oil for frying
- Toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Slice the eggplant lengthwise
2. Blanch the eggplant slices briefly in boiling water
3. In the middle of each eggplant slice, place a slice of avocado and a slice of red pepper (or any filling you prefer)
4. Roll up and secure with toothpicks
5. Make a batter with flour and water
6. Dip the eggplant rolls in the batter
7. Roll in chopped or ground almonds
8. Fry until golden and crispy
