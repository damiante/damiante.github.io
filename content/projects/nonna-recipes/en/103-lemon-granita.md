# Granita / Lemon Granita

## Ingredients

- Juice of 4 lemons (or ½ cup lemon juice)
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ litre water

## Method

1. Boil the water with the sugar for 5 minutes
2. Let cool completely
3. Add the lemon juice (which should already be squeezed)
4. Break into small pieces
5. Process in a food processor for 3-4 minutes
6. Put in an ice cream container and place in the deep freeze
7. When it begins to freeze (about an hour), remove and process again in the food processor for about 18 minutes
8. Return to freezer
9. Eat after 1-2 days
