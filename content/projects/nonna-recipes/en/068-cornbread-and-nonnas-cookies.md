# Cornbread and Nonna's Cookies

## Cornbread (Pane di Granturco)

### Ingredients
- 1½ cups cornmeal
- ½ cup flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- ¼ cup oil

### Instructions
1. Mix well
2. Bake for 20 minutes at 200°C

## Nonna's Cookies (Biscotti di Nona)

### Ingredients
- 4 cups plain flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 pound butter
- 6 eggs
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- Zest of 2 lemons

### Instructions
1. Beat sugar and butter to a cream
2. Add eggs one at a time and beat well after each egg
3. Add flour little by little
4. Add baking powder, vanilla, and lemon zest
5. Bake at 350°F (time not specified)
