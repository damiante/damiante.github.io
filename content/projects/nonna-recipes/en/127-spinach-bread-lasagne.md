# Lasagne su con Spinaci / Spinach Bread Lasagne

## Ingredients

- 10 slices homemade bread
- 400 grams mascarpone
- 1 leek
- 100 grams grated cheese
- 1 glass milk
- 1 kilogram spinach

## Method

1. Boil the spinach and leek together
2. Mix the cooked spinach and leek with the mascarpone
3. Grease a baking dish
4. Dip bread slices in milk
5. Layer: bread soaked in milk, then spinach mixture, then grated cheese
6. Continue layering until all ingredients are finished
7. Bake at 180°C

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
