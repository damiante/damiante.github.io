# Cache / Chocolate Marble Cake

## Ingredients

- 5 eggs
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 glass seed oil
- 1½ cups self-raising flour
- Lemon zest
- ½ cup cocoa powder dissolved in a little coffee or liqueur

## Method

1. Beat the egg whites with the sugar
2. Add the egg yolks
3. Add the oil
4. Add the flour
5. Divide the batter in half
6. To one half, add the dissolved cocoa (dissolved in coffee or liqueur)
7. Pour half the plain batter into a greased pan
8. Pour the chocolate batter on top in the middle
9. Bake at 320°F (160°C) for approximately 30-35 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 320°F (160°C)
