# Salame Turco (Turkish Salami)

## Ingredients
- 300 grams cookies/biscuits, broken into small pieces
- 100 grams butter, melted
- 3 eggs
- 40 grams cocoa
- 200 grams sugar

## Instructions
1. Mix cocoa and sugar together first
2. Beat the eggs
3. Mix sugar and cocoa together
4. Beat well
5. Beat the egg whites separately and mix
6. Add the broken biscuits
7. Mix everything together
8. Roll in **carta di silva** (likely parchment paper or wax paper)
9. Place in the refrigerator
10. The butter should be added cold
