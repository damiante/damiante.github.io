# Polpette di Latte e Semolina / Semolina and Vegetable Patties

## Ingredients

- 600 ml milk
- 120 grams semolina
- Carrots, cut into small pieces
- Onion, cut into small pieces
- Broccoli, cut into small pieces
- Celery, cut into small pieces
- Parmesan cheese, grated
- Oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Put the milk in a pot
2. When hot, add the semolina
3. Stir continuously until it forms a thick cream
4. Fry all the vegetables (carrots, onion, broccoli, celery) in a pan
5. Mix the fried vegetables with the semolina cream
6. Spread the mixture in a dish and let cool
7. Form into patties
8. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese on top
9. Bake at 300째F (150째C) for 30 minutes
