# Biscotti Lolo

## Ingredients

### For the Cookies
- 4 eggs
- 1 can Nestle condensed milk
- Self-raising flour, as much as it takes
- 1½ cups sugar
- 2 cups cold water

## Instructions

1. Beat the eggs with the condensed milk
2. Add self-raising flour gradually until it forms a dough
3. Shape the cookies as desired
4. Bake until golden

### For the Sugar Glaze
1. Combine 1½ cups sugar with 2 cups cold water in a pot
2. Bring to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes
3. Pour the hot syrup over the baked cookies
4. Turn the cookies constantly in the syrup to coat evenly

**Oven Temperature:** Not specified
