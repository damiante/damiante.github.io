# Torrone (Italian Nougat)

## Ingredients
- 5 egg whites
- 1 kilogram cleaned and toasted almonds
- 2 packets hazelnuts
- Half kilogram (or 450 grams) honey
- Half kilogram caster sugar
- **Bosanne** a little of (unclear ingredient)
- 1 tablespoon (or teaspoon) vanilla

## Instructions
1. Beat the egg whites for a little while
2. Then add the sugar
3. And beat well for 15 minutes
4. Then cook in a double boiler (bain-marie) for one hour and fifteen minutes (or 8-10 minutes according to another version)
5. And then add the almonds (and hazelnuts)
6. Cut the almonds into pieces
7. Then put it in the dish
8. Put waxed paper underneath and on top
9. Press it a little
10. And put weight on top
11. Then cut it as you wish

**NOTE:** There are several variants with slightly different times and quantities.
