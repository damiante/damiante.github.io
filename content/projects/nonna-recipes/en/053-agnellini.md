# Agnellini

## Ingredients
- 3 whole eggs
- 1 packet (500 grams) raisins
- 370 grams plain flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon ground cloves
- 1 sachet vanilla powder (optional, not necessary)

## Instructions
1. Beat the eggs with a mixer
2. Add the raisins
3. Add the flour by hand with a wooden spoon
4. **Sabastiari** (mix/knead)
5. Shape them long and make marks with a fork
6. Or shape them like little doves (**colombella piccola**)
7. Bake at 180Â°C
8. After baking, they must rest for 1 day or 2 days
