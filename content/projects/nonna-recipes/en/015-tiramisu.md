# Tiramisu

## Ingredients
- 1 packet sponge finger biscuits (ladyfingers)
- Cocoa to dust
- Coffee
- 300ml cream
- 2 egg yolks
- 120 grams sugar
- 100 grams soft cream cheese (Italian style)
- 100 grams mascarpone cheese (Italian)
- Dash vanilla essence
- Sambuca to taste

## Instructions
1. Mix all cream ingredients together
2. Soak biscuits in coffee and place one layer on tray
3. Cover layer with Â½ of cream mixture
4. Place another layer of soaked biscuits
5. Cover with rest of cream
6. Dust top with cocoa
7. Leave for 24 hours before serving
