# Patate Ripieni / Stuffed Potatoes

## Ingredients

- 4 normal potatoes
- 60 grams soft cheese
- 60 grams ricotta
- 2 eggs
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Boil the potatoes until half-cooked
2. Let cool, then cut in half
3. Scoop out some of the potato flesh
4. Beat the eggs separately (whites and yolks)
5. Mix the soft cheese and ricotta with the beaten eggs
6. Fill the potato halves with the cheese mixture
7. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes or until golden

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
