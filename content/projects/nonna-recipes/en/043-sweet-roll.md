# Sweet Roll

## Ingredients
- 3 eggs
- 100 grams sugar
- 50 grams flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons butter
- 50 grams potato starch

### Filling
- **Macaronello** or Nutella

## Instructions
1. Beat eggs and sugar together
2. Add everything else and mix
3. Bake at 180°C for 13 minutes
4. **Tigliando tutto a giro** (cut all around)
5. Spread Nutella or **macaronello** inside
6. Roll it up
