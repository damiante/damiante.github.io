# Biscotti con Carote / Carrot Cookies (Two Versions)

## Version 1: Soft Carrot Almond Cookies

### Ingredients
- 200 grams sugar
- 4 carrots, grated
- 2 eggs
- 90 ml seed oil
- 100 ml milk
- 70 grams ground almonds
- Zest of 1 lemon, grated
- Flour, as much as needed (should be slightly soft)

### Instructions
1. Beat eggs and sugar together
2. Add grated carrots, oil, milk, ground almonds, and lemon zest
3. Mix well
4. Add flour gradually until the dough is slightly soft
5. Place on parchment paper
6. Bake at 200째C for 12 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 200째C

## Version 2: Carrot Hazelnut Cookies

### Ingredients
- 100 grams sugar
- 2 carrots, grated
- 150 grams butter
- 1 egg
- A little cinnamon
- 30 grams flour
- 100 grams ground hazelnuts
- A little milk

### Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Let rest in refrigerator for 1 hour
3. Roll out the dough and cut into desired shapes using a cookie cutter
4. Sprinkle a little sugar on top
5. Bake at 180째C until golden

**Oven Temperature:** 180째C
