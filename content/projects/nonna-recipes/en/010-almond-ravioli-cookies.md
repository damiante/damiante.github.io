# Almond Ravioli Cookies

## Ingredients
- 1 kilogram almonds
- 1 kilogram sugar
- 4 egg whites
- A little extra egg whites for mixing
- 2 tablespoons honey

## Instructions
1. Mix almonds, sugar (spinale), and a little egg whites
2. Pass them through the machine twice
3. Add 2 tablespoons of honey
4. Then beat the egg whites with the sugar
5. Then mix them with the machine
6. Then press them into cornflour
7. Beat the egg whites with the sugar
8. And then mix them with the machine
