# Muffin o Ciello / Ricotta Sultana Muffins

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 100 grams brown sugar
- 150 ml milk
- 130 grams butter
- 320 grams ricotta
- 500 grams flour
- 200 grams sultanas (raisins)

## Instructions

1. Beat sugar and butter together
2. Add eggs and beat well
3. Mix in ricotta
4. Add flour and sultanas
5. Mix everything together
6. Fill muffin tins
7. Bake at 320째F (160째C) for 28 minutes
