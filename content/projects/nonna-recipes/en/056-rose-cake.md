# Torta alle Rose (Rose Cake)

## Ingredients
- 500 grams self-raising flour (better without yeast)
- 170 grams sugar
- 170 grams butter
- 5 eggs (4 yolks and 1 whole)
- 30 grams fresh yeast
- 1 glass milk
- Small piece of salt

## Instructions
1. Mix flour with milk, yeast, butter, 50g sugar, and salt
2. Let rest for half an hour
3. Roll out like lasagna (thin)
4. Mix remaining sugar and butter together
5. Spread on the pasta
6. Roll it up round
7. Cut into pieces
8. Close the bottom
9. Place one piece in the center of the dish
10. Arrange all other pieces around it in a circle
11. Should make 9 pieces in total
12. Let it rest for half an hour when you put it in the dish
13. When pulling the pieces apart, place a little parchment paper underneath
14. Bake at 180Â°C for half an hour
