# Persimmon Jam / Marmellata di Cachi

## Ingredients

- 3 cups persimmon pulp
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon grated lemon zest

## Method

1. Clean the persimmons
2. Process them in a food processor until smooth
3. Cook over low heat for 20-25 minutes
4. Check that it's thick enough
5. When you remove it from the heat, add the lemon juice and grated zest
6. Pour into sterilized jars while hot
7. Seal immediately
