# Dolce di Prugne / Plum Cake

## Ingredients

- 800 grams plums, cut into small pieces
- 4 egg whites
- 100 grams raw sugar
- 120 grams flour (half self-raising, half plain)
- 1 tablespoon cocoa
- 1 packet vanilla
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon
- 100 ml milk

## Method

1. Beat the egg whites and sugar until fluffy
2. When well beaten, add the rest of the ingredients
3. Finally add the milk
4. Fold in the chopped plums
5. Pour into a greased pan
6. Bake at 190°C for 30-40 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 190°C
