# Cheesecake

## Ingredients
### Crust
- 1 packet Nabisco **bobin** shortbread biscuits
- 90 grams butter
- 1 tablespoon cinnamon or nutmeg

### Filling
- 1 tin Nestle condensed milk
- 1 packet cream cheese
- Â½ cup fresh lemon juice

### Topping
- 1 tablespoon gelatin
- 1 tablespoon hot water
- **Cahese** (cherries?) for topping

## Instructions
1. Crush the biscuits
2. Mix with butter and cinnamon
3. Press into a dish
4. Place in the refrigerator
5. Beat the cream cheese
6. Mix in the Nestle condensed milk
7. Add lemon juice
8. Pour over the biscuit base
9. Return to refrigerator
10. Dissolve gelatin in hot water
11. Pour over the cheese layer
12. Top with cherries
13. Refrigerate until set
