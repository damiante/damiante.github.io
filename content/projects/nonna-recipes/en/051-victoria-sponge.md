# Victoria Sponge

## Ingredients
- 3 eggs (whole)
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup cornflour
- ½ tablespoon plain flour
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar

## Instructions
1. Beat 3 eggs together (yolks and whites)
2. Add 1 cup of sugar
3. Beat for 5 to 6 minutes
4. Sift the cornflour, plain flour, and cream of tartar through a sieve three times
5. Mix all the dry ingredients together
6. Mix with the eggs
7. Bake at 375°F
