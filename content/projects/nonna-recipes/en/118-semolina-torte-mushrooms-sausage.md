# Tortella di Semolino / Semolina Torte with Mushrooms and Sausage

## Ingredients

- 1 litre milk
- 250 grams semolina
- 70 grams grated cheese
- 50 grams butter
- 200 grams mushrooms
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 sausages

## Method

1. Heat the milk and add the butter
2. When hot, add the semolina
3. Stir continuously to make a thick mixture
4. Let cool
5. Mix in the egg yolks and grated cheese
6. Spread the mixture out and let cool completely
7. Cut into rounds
8. Place the rounds in a baking dish
9. Fry the mushrooms in a pan
10. Fry the sausages and slice them
11. Place the mushrooms and sausage slices on top
12. Sprinkle with more grated cheese
13. Bake until golden
