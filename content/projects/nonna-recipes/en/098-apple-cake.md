# Torta di Mele / Apple Cake

## Ingredients

- 150 grams sugar
- 150 grams self-raising flour
- 150 grams butter
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 4 eggs
- Zest of 1 orange, grated
- 4 apples
- 50 grams almonds (optional)
- 50 grams hazelnuts or pine nuts (optional)
- 125 grams cognac (optional)

## Instructions

1. Beat together sugar, eggs, and lemon zest
2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
3. Cut the apples into small slices
4. Add the sliced apples to the mixture
5. Fold in the flour
6. If using, add almonds, hazelnuts or pine nuts, and cognac
7. Mix everything together well
8. Pour onto parchment paper and place in a greased dish
9. If desired, make extra dough and place strips on top
10. Sprinkle a little sugar on top
11. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes

**Oven Temperature:** 180°C
